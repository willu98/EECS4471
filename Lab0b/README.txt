Translation controlled by clicking keys w, a, d, x
Rotation on y axis controlled by clicking a, d 
Rotation on x axis controlled by clicking w, x
Toggling between rotation and translation is controlled by clicking r or t

There are 3 game objects that can be replicated and destroyed, a sphere, capsule, and cube.
Clicking s switches between the objects, which is indicated by a red color.
Clicking n creates randomly positioned objects and k destroys any of the objects.

Low poly fps pack
